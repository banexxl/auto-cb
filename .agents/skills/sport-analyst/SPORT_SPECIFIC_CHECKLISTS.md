# Sport-Specific Checklists

## Football / Soccer
Check:

- Starting XI and bench
- Injuries, illness, suspensions
- Goalkeeper availability
- Defensive line stability
- Fixture congestion
- European/domestic rotation risk
- Home/away performance
- Set pieces
- Pressing and transition defense
- Motivation: title, Europe, relegation, cup priority
- Referee card/penalty tendencies
- Weather and pitch condition

## Basketball
Check:

- Injury report and minutes restrictions
- Back-to-back games
- Travel fatigue
- Usage rate changes when stars are out
- Rebounding matchup
- Three-point volume and defense
- Pace
- Bench depth
- Foul trouble risk
- Motivation and standings

## Tennis
Check:

- Surface preference
- Recent workload
- Injury/illness news
- Head-to-head style matchup
- Serve/return stats
- Break-point performance
- Fatigue from previous rounds
- Travel and climate adaptation
- Motivation and tournament priority

## American Football
Check:

- Quarterback status
- Offensive line injuries
- Defensive injuries by unit
- Weather/wind
- Rest days
- Travel
- Coaching tendencies
- Run/pass matchup
- Turnover profile
- Special teams

## Ice Hockey
Check:

- Starting goalie
- Back-to-back schedule
- Defensive pair injuries
- Power play / penalty kill
- Shot quality
- Travel fatigue
- Recent overtime workload
- Home/away splits

## Baseball
Check:

- Starting pitcher
- Bullpen workload
- Lineup handedness matchup
- Weather/wind
- Travel
- Rest days
- Recent batting form
- Defensive absences

## Combat Sports
Check:

- Weight cut
- Injury rumors and camp news
- Style matchup
- Reach/stance
- Grappling vs striking advantage
- Cardio and pace
- Short-notice replacement
- Judging/referee tendencies

## Esports
Check:

- Current patch/meta
- Roster changes
- Recent scrim/public performance, if reliable
- Map pool
- Side selection
- Travel/LAN vs online
- Player illness
- Motivation and tournament format
- Integrity history and official sanctions
